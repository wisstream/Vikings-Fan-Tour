Viking is a Canadian-Irish series currently fully available on Netflix created by Michael Hirst, broadcast between March 3rd 2013 and December 30th 2020 on History channel in Canada and History channel in the United States.

The easiest way to do this fan tour is to fly from one airport to another, with a budget of around 1500€ (+ about 100€/day life's expensive in those countries) for flights and accommodation.

Places

Vardø, Norway

Vardo is one of the key towns in the series and a beautiful place to visit, especially for the Drakkar Viking memorial.

Useful expressions: Good Morning / Good evening, Good Bye, Welcome Translation: Hallo, Ha det, Velkommen.

Reykjavík, Iceland

Reykjavík, the capital of Iceland and a city known for its freezing climate, is also one of the outstanding cities and villages in the series.

Fun expression: Don't let your grief get higher than your knees. Translation: Láttu kvörtun þína ekki verða hærri en hnén.

Falaise, France

How not to mention a french place, especially as it is where the main character's brother, Rollo lodbrok, lived.

Emblematic idioms: Impossible isn't french, pushing granny into the nettles Translation: Impossible n'est pas français, pousser mémé dans les orties

Stockholm, Sweden

Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, known for Zlatan Ibrahimovic, its lake region but also and above all for Bjorn's grave.

Very funny idiom: There's no cow on the ice, Caught the beard in the mailbox Translation: Det finns ingen ko på isen, Fick skägget i brevlådan
